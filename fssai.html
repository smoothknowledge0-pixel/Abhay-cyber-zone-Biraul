Frequently Asked Questions

Clear Your Doubts

Q1. FSSAI Licence kya hota hai?
Yeh food business run karne ke liye ek mandatory Government approval aur food safety certificate hai.
Q2. Kya chhote business ke liye zaruri hai?
Haan, chhota ho ya bada, har food business ke liye basic FSSAI registration mandatory hai.
Q3. Kitne types hote hain?
Teen (3) types hote hain: Basic Registration, State Licence, aur Central Licence (turnover ke hisaab se).
Q4. Kitne din me milta hai?
Basic certificate 5-7 working days me aur State/Central 15-30 days me generate ho jata hai.
Q5. Validity kitni hoti hai?
FSSAI Licence ki validity aap 1 saal se lekar 5 saal tak select kar sakte hain.
Q6. Licence na hone par kya hoga?
Bina FSSAI ke business chalana illegal hai, jisme bhaari penalty lag sakti hai aur business band ho sakta hai.
Q7. Online apply hota hai?
Haan, hamara process 100% online hai, aap ghar baithe WhatsApp ke through apply karwa sakte hain.
Q8. GST zaruri hai?
Nahi, basic FSSAI ke liye GST optional hai, but bade business ke liye mandatory ho sakta hai.
Q9. Home kitchen apply kar sakta hai?
Haan, bilkul. Agar aap ghar se khana banakar bechte hain, tab bhi basic FSSAI registration zaruri hai.
Q10. Renewal kab karna hota hai?
Licence expiry date se pehle renewal karwana zaroori hota hai, warna late fine lag sakta hai.
Q11. Khud apply kar sakte hain?
Haan, but FOSCOS portal complex ho sakta hai. Galat details dalne se application reject ho sakti hai.
Q12. Aapka process kya hai?
Aap huye documents share karein, hum draft banake apply karenge, aur approval tak full support denge.
