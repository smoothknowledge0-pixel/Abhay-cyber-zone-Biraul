Frequently Asked Questions

Clear Your Doubts

Udyam Registration kya hai?
Yeh Government of India ka official MSME certificate hai jo chhote/bade business ko legal recognition deta hai.
Kya ye compulsory hai?
Compulsory nahi hai, lekin current account open karne, loan lene aur govt benefits ke liye highly recommended hai.
Kya bina GST ke ban sakta hai?
Haan, aap bina GST number ke bhi aasaani se Udyam registration ke liye apply kar sakte hain.
Kya student apply kar sakta hai?
Haan, agar koi student freelance work, blogging, ya koi chhota business run kar raha hai toh wo apply kar sakta hai.
Kitne time me certificate milta hai?
Mostly same day ya processing clear hone par 24 hours ke andar Udyam certificate mil jata hai.
Kya ye lifetime valid hai?
Haan, Udyam certificate lifetime valid hota hai, isme kisi tarah ka renewal required nahi hota.
Kya loan me help karta hai?
Bilkul, Mudra loan aur government MSME schemes/subsidy ke liye banks Udyam registration sabse pehle maangte hain.
Kya khud apply kar sakte hain?
Haan, portal open hai but NIC code error ya details mistake se bachne ke liye expert help best rehti hai.
Aadhaar par multiple registration?
Nahi, ek Aadhaar par ek hi registration hota hai. Agar business multiple hain toh usi ek certificate me add kiye jate hain.
Kya ye free hai?
Govt portal fee zero hai. Humara minor charge sirf professional form filling, expert guidance aur support ka hai.
