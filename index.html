GST Registration Kya Hai?

GST (Goods and Services Tax) ek indirect tax hai jo pure India me businesses ke liye mandatory hai. Ye aapke business ko legal identity deta hai.

  • Business ko legal identity milti hai
  • B2B invoice generate kar sakte hain
  • Amazon, Flipkart par online selling ke liye zaroori
  • Input Tax Credit (ITC) ka fayda milta hai
